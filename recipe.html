step 1
First put the sugar and butter in the bowl, then the grated lemon zest, the flower and the eggs after that mix it.

step 2
Then knead the dough by hand and let it cool for 1-2 hours.

step 3
Cut out the dough using the cutters provided and place them on a baking sheet.

step 4
Bake the dough with hot air at 200 degrees for 10 minutes and then you're done!
